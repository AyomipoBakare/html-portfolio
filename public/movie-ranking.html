The Best Movies According to Ayomipo

My top 3 movies of all time.

Hackshaw Ridge

The true story of Pfc. Desmond T. Doss (Andrew Garfield), who won the Congressional Medal of Honor despite refusing to bear arms during WWII on religious grounds. Doss was drafted and ostracized by fellow soldiers for his pacifist stance but went on to earn respect and adoration for his bravery, selflessness and compassion after he risked his life -- without firing a shot -- to save 75 men in the Battle of Okinawa.

It's a story of perseverance, courage and sheer fortitude, and one bound to affect even the most hardened viewer.

Release date: 16 October 2016 (Australia)

Director: Mel Gibson

The Hangover (Series)

The Hangover" is a funny movie, flat out, all the way through. Its setup is funny. Every situation is funny. Most of the dialogue is funny.

The Hangover is an outrageous comedy with non-stop laughs.. its three parts are equally Wild and crazy.

Release date: 2 June 2009 (USA)

Director: Todd Phillips

Shawshank Redemption

Andy Dufresne, a successful banker, is arrested for the murders of his wife and her lover, and is sentenced to life imprisonment at the Shawshank prison. He becomes the most unconventional prisoner.

This movie is sad, uplifting, inspiring, harsh, cold, funny (at the right times), jaw-dropping, and heart-warming all at the same time. I get chills every time

Release date: 22 September 1994 (Beverly Hills)

Director: Frank Darabont
